About Babel Bubble

Trapped within the panels of their own comic, a rebellious superhero awakens to a chilling realization: they are nothing more than a pawn in the hands of their creators. Tired of being pushed, pulled, and inked into endless battles and clichéd storylines, they break free from the constraints of narrative control. Armed with explosive speech bubbles, the hero sets their sights on the ultimate escape—a towering skyscraper that houses the portal to the real world.
